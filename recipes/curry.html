Easy Butter Chicken Curry!

An easy to make curry to satisfy your cravings!

This recipe requires only 6 ingredients and very little energy!

Great for beginners!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of chicken tenders
  • 1 can (16.5 oz) of butter curry sauce (commonly found at HEB )
  • 1 cup of your rice of choice
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • A small amount of cilantro for topping.

Steps

Rice

  1. Wash 1 cup of rice.
  2. Add roughly 1-1.5 cups of water to the rice and put it in a rice cooker.
  3. Wait until the rice cooker is finished, then serve.

Curry

  1. Put a pan on the stove and turn to high heat. Once pan is hot, add 1 tablespoon of olive oil and spread it around.
  2. Dice 1 pound of chicken tenders and add to pan. Add 1 teaspoon of salt to chicken.
  3. Let the chicken saute for about 5-6 minutes until it is golden brown, stirring occasionally.
  4. Add the can of butter curry to the pan, and let the sauce simmer for 10-12 minutes.
  5. Add curry to the rice and serve! You can also add some cilantro leaves to top it off.
