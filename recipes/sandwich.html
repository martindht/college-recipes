Customizable Vietnamese Sandwich (Banh Mi)

A great option if you're in a hurry and need to pack food to go!

This recipe only requires a few essential ingredients, which are bolded! Everything else is is customizable for your needs.

Great for when you need to pack food to eat during those study sessions in the library!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices bread or a small baguette
  • Mayonnaise (the chili version if you want something more spicy)
  • Pâté (any type is fine - but I find pork to work the best)
  • Vietnamese ham (also called Chả lụa)
  • Your desired vegetables (common ones used include sliced pickled carrots/daikon, cilantro, cucumber, chilis/jalenpeño
  • Your desired additional protein (common ones include shredded chicken and grilled beef)
  • Anything you think will work well with the initial ingredients :D

Steps

  1. Toast 2 slices of bread/baguette.
  2. Spread mayonnaise on 1 slice and pâté on the other to amount desired.
  3. Slice the Vietnamese Ham and add to the sandwich - again with the amount desired.
  4. Throw whatever else you want on there and serve!
