Pasta with Marinara Sauce!

An easy and delicious pasta that can be made quickly!

This recipe is great for beginners and takes less than half an hour to make!

It is very flavorful but doesn't require many ingredients!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 1 can (24 oz) of marinara pasta sauce (commonly found at HEB)
  • 12 oz of veggie pasta
  • 2 teaspoons of salt
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon of italian seasoning
  • Parmesan cheese to your liking!

Steps

Pasta

  1. Boil roughly 4 quarts of water in a pot.
  2. Add 1 teaspoon of salt and 1 tablespoon of olive oil after water is boiled.
  3. Add pasta to the pot and let it cook for 7-10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Drain pasta.

Sauce

  1. Put a pan on the stove and turn to medium high heat. Once pan is hot, add 1 tablespoon of olive oil and spread it around.
  2. Add 1 pound of ground beef to pan. Break it up into smaller chunks. Add 1 teaspoon of salt to ground beef.
  3. Let ground beef cook for about 5-6 minutes until it is brown, stirring often.
  4. Add the can of marinara sauce to the pan, and let the sauce simmer for 10-12 minutes.
  5. Add the italian seasoning and serve with the pasta! Add parmesan cheese to your liking!
